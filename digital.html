Detailed Description

"Manage menus with photos & prices online" describes a web-based system that empowers business owners and staff to keep their menu content up-to-date across all customer touchpoints. This includes adding new dishes, enriching listings with high-quality photos, adjusting prices, scheduling availability, and marking items as seasonal or sold out.

How it works (step-by-step)

  1. Login to a secure admin dashboard.
  2. Create a new menu item: name, category, description, price, SKU (optional).
  3. Upload one or more photos and choose the primary display image.
  4. Set availability, portion size, and optional modifiers (e.g., extra toppings, spice level).
  5. Publish — updates are pushed instantly to your public menu (QR menu, website, app).
